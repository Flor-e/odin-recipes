Stamppot

Stamppot is a very famous Dutch recipe in which you vigorously mash a vegetable of choice together with potatoes. You can basically use any type of vegetable you like, but often used are sauerkraut, carrots or boerenkool.

This recipe just gives you a basic idea of how we like to make stamppot at home.

Ingredients

  • Lots of potatoes
  • Lots of a vegetable of choice
  • Cup of sour cream
  • Lots of bacon
  • Large onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • Milk
  • Grated cheese
  • Sausage!

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes soft
  2. Do the same with the veggie of choice
  3. Bake the bacon, Take out of the pan. Bake onion and later garlic in the bacon fat.
  4. Take a big pan, mash potatoes and veggies and add all other ingredients.
  5. And do not forget the sausage!
